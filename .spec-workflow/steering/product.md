# Product Steering: Zella Trade Scribe

## 1. Product Vision

To become the leading forex-specific trading journal that empowers retail and professional traders with institutional-grade analytics, risk management, and market intelligence. Zella Trade Scribe will transform how traders analyze and improve their performance by providing accessible, specialized tools tailored to the nuances of currency markets.

## 2. Target Audience

- **Primary**: Retail and professional forex traders seeking to improve performance through data-driven insights.
- **Secondary**: Trading educators, analysts, and risk managers focused on currency markets.

## 3. Core Problems to Solve

- **Lack of Forex-Specific Analytics**: Generic tools don't understand currency correlations, pip analysis, or economic event impacts on forex.
- **Poor Risk Management**: Traders lack real-time, actionable risk assessment tools.
- **Fragmented Market Intelligence**: Economic news and analysis are disconnected from the trading journal.
- **Inadequate Performance Analytics**: Basic P&L tracking without meaningful insights for improvement.

## 4. Key Features & Functionality

- **Forex-Specific Analytics**: Currency pair correlation, pip distribution analysis, and currency strength meters.
- **Advanced Risk Management**: Real-time portfolio risk dashboard, leverage calculators, and drawdown analysis.
- **Economic Intelligence Integration**: Integrated economic calendar with trade impact analysis.
- **Comprehensive Journaling**: Detailed trade logging, emotional state tracking, and template management.
- **Performance Reporting**: In-depth session analysis, professional visualizations, and exportable insights.
- **Data Management**: Secure, multi-format data export (PDF, JSON, CSV) and automated backup/restore functionality.

## 5. User Outcomes

- **Improved Trading Decisions**: Users will make more informed decisions based on comprehensive, forex-specific data.
- **Enhanced Risk Control**: Users will better manage risk through professional-grade tools and real-time insights.
- **Increased Profitability**: By identifying patterns and improving discipline, users will enhance their trading performance.
- **Greater Emotional Discipline**: Users will understand and manage the psychological aspects of trading through dedicated tracking and analysis.

## 6. Success Metrics

- **Feature Completeness**: All core features implemented to a professional standard.
- **User Adoption & Engagement**: High retention rates and active daily use of journaling and analytics features.
- **Business Readiness**: Successful rollout of a premium tier with advanced features.
- **Competitive Positioning**: Recognized as a feature-rich, cost-effective alternative to expensive institutional platforms.
