# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
Zella Trade Scribe is a specialized futures trading journal and risk management platform designed specifically for prop firm challenge participants and futures traders. The platform transforms how traders approach funded trading evaluations by providing institutional-grade analytics, real-time risk monitoring, and challenge-specific compliance tools tailored to the unique requirements of futures markets and prop firm programs.

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Prop firm challenge participants seeking to pass funded trading evaluations and secure funded accounts
- **Secondary**: Professional futures traders, retail futures traders, and trading educators focused on futures markets
- **Tertiary**: Prop firm evaluators and risk managers who need to assess trader performance

## Key Features

1. **Prop Firm Challenge Management**: Complete challenge tracking with rule compliance monitoring, progress visualization, and automated pass/fail notifications
2. **Futures-Specific Trade Analytics**: Specialized tracking for contract sizes, tick values, margin requirements, and exchange-specific data
3. **Real-Time Risk Dashboard**: Live drawdown monitoring, position sizing alerts, and risk capacity tracking tailored to challenge requirements
4. **Challenge Rule Engine**: Automated compliance checking for trading hours, position limits, news trading restrictions, and other prop firm rules
5. **Futures Market Data Integration**: Real-time price feeds, volume analysis, and open interest tracking for major futures exchanges
6. **Performance Analytics**: Challenge-specific metrics, strategy optimization tools, and detailed completion reports

## Business Objectives

- Capture the growing prop firm challenge market by providing specialized tools unavailable in generic trading journals
- Establish market leadership in futures trading journal software through specialized features and deep market understanding
- Build a sustainable business model around premium challenge tracking and advanced analytics features
- Create a community platform that supports traders through their funded trading journey

## Success Metrics

- **User Adoption**: 1,000+ active prop firm challenge participants within 6 months
- **Challenge Success Rate**: 40%+ pass rate for users actively using the platform (vs. industry average of 10-15%)
- **Feature Engagement**: 80%+ of users actively using risk dashboard and challenge tracking features
- **Revenue Growth**: $50K+ ARR from premium features within 12 months

## Product Principles

1. **Challenge-First Design**: Every feature is designed with prop firm challenge requirements as the primary consideration
2. **Futures Market Expertise**: Deep understanding of futures trading mechanics, margin systems, and exchange-specific nuances
3. **Risk Management Focus**: Real-time risk monitoring and prevention tools are core to the user experience
4. **Compliance Automation**: Minimize manual rule checking through intelligent automation and alerts
5. **Performance Optimization**: Help traders not just track performance, but actively improve their challenge success rates

## Monitoring & Visibility

- **Dashboard Type**: Web-based responsive dashboard with mobile optimization
- **Real-time Updates**: WebSocket connections for live market data and risk calculations
- **Key Metrics Displayed**: Daily/overall drawdown, profit target progress, rule compliance status, position risk
- **Sharing Capabilities**: Challenge completion reports, performance analytics exports, and risk assessment summaries
